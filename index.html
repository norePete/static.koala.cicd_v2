Why this exists

Are you a paranoid schizophrenic? Have you already declared yourself a sovereign citizen and buried gold bars in your back yard? Me too baby, me too. But sometimes I feel like I want a bank account that isn't at any risk of collapse.

For most of my daily needs I use cash, I rarely use my bank account. But sometimes I want to be able to deposit my paycheck into an account without participating in the fractional reserve banking system that has been destroying our economy. That's why virtual nzd exists, deposit cash or pay with a credit card and hold your own virtual dollars in a decentralized banking system denominated in new zealand dollars. This is not a stable coin, it is an asymmetric asset with a floating exchange rate.

Use decentralized exchanges to convert between virtual nzd and anything from Bitcoin to USDT, the echange rate on these exchanges is set automatically by the supply and demand curves for each currency, and in some cases you may be able to get a better deal buying through an exchange compared to buying through this payment gateway. We will always charge exactly $1 for 1 nzd. We only provide a fiat on-ramp, we DO NOT provide any method of cashing back out of nzd into either cash or bank deposits, the virtual nzd is not intended to be a separate asset class to physical cash. As such we do not try to peg the cash out exchange rate to $1, and discourage holding this asset in the hopes of making money via speculation.

If you do need to cash out you can use any centralized exchange that offers nzd deposits, or via an over-the-counter transactions with a private buyer.
